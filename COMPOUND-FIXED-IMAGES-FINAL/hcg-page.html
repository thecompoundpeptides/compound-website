Overview

Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (HCG) is a naturally occurring hormone that plays crucial roles in both male and female reproductive health. In therapeutic applications, HCG is used to stimulate natural testosterone production in men, enhance fertility in both sexes, and support weight loss protocols.

HCG mimics luteinizing hormone (LH), signaling the testes to produce testosterone and preventing testicular atrophy during testosterone replacement therapy. It's also essential for maintaining fertility and can be used as part of comprehensive hormone optimization protocols.
