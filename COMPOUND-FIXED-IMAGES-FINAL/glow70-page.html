Safety & Side Effects

Glow70 has an excellent safety profile with minimal side effects:

Common Effects (Typically Mild)

  • Temporary redness at injection site
  • Mild tingling sensation
  • Initial skin purging (detox effect)
  • Increased photosensitivity initially

Precautions

  • Use SPF 30+ sunscreen daily during treatment
  • Stay well-hydrated to support skin renewal
  • Avoid harsh skincare products initially
  • Gradual introduction recommended for sensitive skin

Contraindications

  • Active skin infections or open wounds
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Active autoimmune skin conditions
  • Recent facial surgery (wait 4 weeks)
  • Keloid scarring tendency
