Getting Started Guide

What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in your body. They can enhance healing, boost metabolism, improve recovery, and support overall health optimization.

How to Choose the Right Peptide

  • For Healing: BPC-157, TB-500
  • For Weight Loss: Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, AOD-9604
  • For Muscle Growth: CJC-1295, Ipamorelin
  • For Longevity: Epithalon, GHK-Cu, NAD+

Storage & Handling

Most peptides require refrigeration after reconstitution. Store unreconstituted peptides in a cool, dark place. Always use sterile techniques when handling.

Safety First

  • Start with lower doses and gradually increase
  • Monitor your body's response
  • Use pharmaceutical-grade products only
  • Consult with a healthcare provider
