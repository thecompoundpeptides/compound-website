NAD+

Essential coenzyme for cellular energy production, DNA repair, and healthy aging

Overview

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a critical coenzyme found in every cell of the body, essential for energy production, DNA repair, and cellular health. NAD+ levels decline by up to 50% as we age, contributing to age-related diseases and reduced cellular function.

Supplementing with NAD+ has been shown to activate sirtuins (longevity genes), improve mitochondrial function, enhance cognitive performance, and potentially slow or reverse aspects of aging at the cellular level.
