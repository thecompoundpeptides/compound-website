Overview

AOD-9604 is a modified fragment of human growth hormone (amino acids 177-191) specifically designed for fat loss. Unlike full HGH, AOD-9604 doesn't affect blood sugar or cause growth effects, making it a safer option for targeted fat reduction.

Originally developed as an anti-obesity drug, AOD-9604 stimulates lipolysis (fat breakdown) and inhibits lipogenesis (fat formation) without the adverse effects associated with HGH use.
